Tonaya Collins






Originally from the Sunshine Coast, Australia, Tonaya spent six years in Beijing, China travelling and studying before moving to San Francisco, CA to finesse her skill at the Academy of Art for three years. There she trained in photography, gaining extensive knowledge of set practices, lighting and post production. Following this she moved to San Jose where her imagination was sparked again by the tech wonders of Silicon Valley. Upon her return to Australia she studied Interactive and Visual Design at Queensland University of Technology, furthering her design and film knowledge with the most recent advances in technology. Currently in Brisbane, Tonaya runs TK Productions. She is available to create immersive videos, narrative photographs and visually stunning designs and style guides. If you are within the Brisbane area, Tonaya is also always looking for fresh faces to shoot and fellow artists to collab with. PH: 0401 724 217 tonayalaa@gmail.com
